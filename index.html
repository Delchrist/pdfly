Frequently asked questions

Are my files really private?
Yes — completely. All processing happens in your browser using JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to a server. Even if you turn off your internet after the page loads, the tools still work.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
Yes. You can cancel from your account dashboard at any time. You'll keep Pro features until the end of your billing period, then revert to the free plan.
What payment methods do you accept?
Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and other local payment methods through Stripe — depending on your country.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes — we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund, no questions asked.

A team plan is coming soon. In the meantime, please email us at hello@pdfly.app for volume pricing.
